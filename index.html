About Clubs

  • Music Club 🎸

    Rhythm is the existence of life; Let it be breathing or heartbeat. Music is all about everything; it gives soul to the universe, and wings to the dream. It is as depth as the ocean, as broad as the sky. It is the path of spirituality that links the soul with the almighty.

  • Quiz Club 🤓

    Our objectives are to increase the level of general awareness amongst the student community, quint essential awareness of Science, Literature, Technology, and Current Affairs outside the realm of the formal syllabus and promote their Intelligence Quotient (IQ) and Quizzing ability.

  • Math Club 🤓

    Our objectives are to spread the love of mathematics to the obscure areas of the world where mathematics is feared and/or disrespected, offer an ideal avenue for a free exchange of mathematical ideas and for frank and helpful criticism of these ideas, promote an interest and awareness of mathematics through a social and intellectual environment, help students in developing heuristic and problem-solving attitude, create a platform for the students to develop intuitive, explorative and creative thinking skills, offer a social outlet for students with a serious interest in mathematics to work on problem solving techniques that go beyond regular classroom curriculum, enjoy mathematics and develop patience and persistence when solving problems, and organize lectures, events and competitions to promote the beauty & applicability of Mathematics.

  • Gardening Club 🌲

    Our objectives are to preserve the environment, increase green-cover & protect the health of public by spreading social awareness on organic farming/gardening, initiate research and development projects with community participation aiming to preserve the environment, to achieve sustainable living, to reduce carbon footprints and to have access to safe and functional food, and create a better world for future generations
